From portfolio to performance

WE MAKE IT EASY!

Bringtune is truly a game-changer for artists looking to showcase their talents and gain recognition within their community. We know that event organizers, wedding planners, corporate event organizers, bars, and restaurants often have a hard time finding a platform that makes it easy to book and manage events and performances.


That's why we're here to take care of all the little details, like sending proposals, and contracts, and handling payments, so you can focus on what you do best - creating art and bringing the fun. Our goal is to bring new opportunities for artists and make the whole process of booking and managing events as smooth and easy as possible.

Quick, Easy, & Free to create your profile

You just need to send us your Instagram username and an email address to receive your score. Make sure your profile is public so our algorithms can analyze public behaviours on your profile. Our algorithms won't access your private information and we never ask you for any sensitive information.

Powerful engine that evaluates all the details

Our AI algorithms will analyze all the public and unique user behaviours on your profile in order to evaluate your influence score between your followers. Our AI brain looks at all the details over time and confidently provides an influence score. Even small details matter to us. The process should not take longer than 48h. In case if you don't receive it on time, we are always here to help you.

Get recognized by showing your influence score on your page

Once you received the result, you can post it on your page and show your followers and public your influence score. Don't forget to come back in a year to get your updated score since our algorithms reevaluate your page on an annual basis.

Cool facts about us

We are the first to analyze user behaviours to provide you with an accurate influence score. Our algorithms are powered by a reach and up-to-date database of carefully selected data points from hundred-thousands pages.
