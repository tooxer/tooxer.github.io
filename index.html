Quick, Easy, & Free to create your profile

Whether you're an artist looking to expand your reach or an event organizer looking for the perfect act, our platform provides the perfect opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals and create unforgettable events.

Focus on the Show, we do the heavy lifting for you!

With our unique proposal system, event organizers can easily send proposals to artists they're interested in working with. Once the artist accepts the proposal, the contract is created and finalized. This streamlined process ensures a hassle-free experience for both parties, allowing them to focus on what really matters: creating unforgettable performances and events.

Secure payment protection for Peace of Mind

We believe both artists and event organizers deserve complete peace of mind when it comes to payments. Bringtune offers a secure payment protection system that holds payments in escrow until the event is successfully completed. This means that both parties can feel confident knowing that payments are protected and that artists will be paid for their exceptional performances.
